How to Register and Use SmileVoice

Complete guide to setting up SmileVoice for affordable calls over 4G LTE

Connectivity Updated December 2024

This guide is essential for Smile data and SmileVoice subscribers. Learn how to download, register, and start making affordable calls using SmileVoice technology.

What is SmileVoice?

SmileVoice is based on Voice over IP (VoIP), also called IP Telephony. It's a method for delivering voice communications and multimedia sessions over Internet Protocol networks such as the Internet.

Key Benefits

  • Make voice calls, video calls, and conference calls
  • Send SMS messages to friends and family
  • Works on any data network connection
  • Lowest call rates in Nigeria

Technology & Longevity

SmileVoice is one of the latest innovations from Smile Communications, making voice experience cheap and reliable. This technology is here to stay as long as there is Wi-Fi connectivity available.

How to Download and Set Up SmileVoice

Step 1: Download the App

Google Play Store Apple App Store

Step 2: Activation

After downloading, you will see the activation screen. Enter the number provided by your Smile Representative and click on "GET AN ACTIVATION CODE".

Step 3: Verification

You will be redirected to a verification page where you enter your registered Smile Number or email address.

Note: This email may be different from your personal email. Contact your Smile Representative for your registered email if needed.

Click Submit. A verification code will be sent to your email or phone number. Copy this code and enter it in the SmileVoice App.

Step 4: Login and Start Calling

Click Login. If successful, your app will show "Ready" status. You can now start calling any network across Nigeria at the lowest rates!
